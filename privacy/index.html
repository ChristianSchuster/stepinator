Privacy Policy

Collected data

Stepinator collects and stores health data locally on your device. If you create an accout and login or login with an existing account, your weekly steps data may be uploaded to our servers to provide the Weekchamp feature.

When you have an account, we store the following on our servers:

  • Email
  • Name (optional, only first name preferred)
  • Avatar image
  • Cumulative weekly steps data

Where is collected data displayed or used

Your email is used as your user name and will be used for password resets. Your name might be displayed within a Weekchamp. Your avatar image might be displayed within a Weekchamp. Your cumulative steps data might be displayed within a Weekchamp and is used to determine your position within a Weekchamp.

Cookies on the site and similar tokens in the app are stored to keep you logged in.

Data exposed to others

When you use Stepinator`s Weekchamp feature your cumulative steps data is uploaded to our servers and updated on all active Weekchamp´s you participate in. Weekchamp´s are like groups, therefor your steps data might be exposed to other members of the Weekchamp´s you participate in.

Requested permissions

Stepinator will request permissions to health data to access steps and distance data.

Stepinator might request permission to access your photos or camera when you want to change your avatar image.

What is uploaded and or stored on our servers

When you create an free account, your email adress is stored on our servers.

When you participate in the Weekchamp feature, your accumulated step count is uploaded and stored on our servers.

Your Consent

By using our site or apps, you consent to our privacy policy.
